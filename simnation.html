Fluid Scalability Across Devices

Built a fluid CSS Grid engine with clamp() typography to handle everything from 1080p laptops to ultra-wide monitors.

Result: 15% error reduction on small screens

Information Density

Applied strict vertical hour scales and high-contrast Slate-300 text to eliminate overlap in 1-hour blocks.

Result: 25% faster schedule verification
